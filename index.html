Sizeable multiplayer room game is a new concept where the fun and social interactivity of traditional board games meets the accessibility and competitiveness of modern MMOG. Games are played with participants located in the same room and teams are formed among friends to win local challenges. The game is played on tablets and smartphones, and the pace is suited for allowing players to socialize with the remaining team elements.

Go out! Meet new people - Instead of turning on your computer and spending the evening alone at home, go to the nearest coffeehouse with your friends and as a team, challenge other players in the same location. You can drink, socialize, meet new people while having fun playing a modern electronic game.

It's the Starbucks© board game for the 21st century.
